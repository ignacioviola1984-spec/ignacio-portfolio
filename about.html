I design, build and deploy AI operating models for finance: multi-agent systems that run close, cash forecasting, reporting and controls, with every figure computed deterministically, a human approving each action, and a full audit trail. The model routes work, drafts explanations and escalates exceptions; it never produces a number on its own.Diseño, construyo y despliego modelos operativos de IA para finanzas: sistemas multiagente que corren cierre, forecast de caja, reporting y controles, con cada cifra calculada de forma determinista, un humano aprobando cada acción y trazabilidad completa. El modelo rutea trabajo, redacta explicaciones y escala excepciones; nunca produce un número por su cuenta.

The judgment behind these systems comes from 17 years in FP&A and Finance Operations at J.P. Morgan, American Express, Accenture and IBM, and at the LATAM fintech unicorns dLocal and Ualá, including building finance functions from scratch in early-stage, high-growth environments.El criterio detrás de estos sistemas viene de 17 años en FP&A y Operaciones Financieras en J.P.
